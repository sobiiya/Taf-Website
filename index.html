About Our Firm

We would like to introduce ourselves as M/s Mirza Agro Foods, India's trusted Exporter of Premium Quality Indian Halal Boneless Buffalo Meat at competitive price.

With a vast and immense experience of 12 years, Mirza Agro Foods is able to establish itself as a leader in Processing Buffalo Meat for exporting them abroad.
