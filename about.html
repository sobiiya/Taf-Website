About Our Firm

We would like to introduce ourselves as M/s Mirza Agro Foods, India's trusted Exporter of Premium Quality Indian Halal Boneless Buffalo Meat at competitive price.

With a vast and immense experience of 12 years, Mirza Agro Foods is able to establish itself as a leader in Processing Buffalo Meat for exporting them abroad. We Process meat for food service companies & consumers across the globe and our products are supplied under Brands “518", “Safa", “Marwah", “Rich", “M8-M8" & “Mirza". Our Buffaloes are raised without the use of hormones & antibiotics that is why our products are more nutritious. We have on Contract 5 Processing facilities and Abattoir which is approved by Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA). At Mirza Agro Foods, we believe in long term business associations with mutual benefits instead of some instant profits and hence we always work in sync with our customers to create a “win-win" business transaction in the light of integrity. With our growing production capabilities & hygienic products, we are exporting to Vietnam, Thailand, West Africa, Egypt, Jordan, Algeria, Lebanon, Iraq, Comoros & GCC Countries. Wish to serve you with our Quality Products with the aim to always strive to serve Better to our Esteemed Customers.

Quality & Food Safety

Ante-mortem and Post-mortem examination is done by Qualified Vetenerary Doctors to ensure that the meat is safe, healthy, unadulterated and free from diseases and contamination.
